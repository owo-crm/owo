# Product Canon

Status: Draft  
Owner: Product  
Last updated: 2026-04-02

## Mission
Build an automation-first CRM for teams buying Meta leads, where first response and next action are always visible.

## Target users
- Lead operators
- Sales owners
- Team leads

## Core outcomes
- Paid leads are not lost between tools.
- First contact happens fast.
- Ownership and next action are explicit.

## Product principles
- One lead, one clear owner.
- Automation should reduce manual clicks, not hide accountability.
- Telegram and Web share one business logic layer.
- Visual complexity must stay lower than operational complexity.

## Success metrics (v1)
- Median time to first response
- Percent of leads with owner assigned within SLA
- Follow-up completion rate
- Conversion rate by pipeline stage

## Non-goals (v1)
- Full enterprise customization
- Complex marketplace/billing variants
- Multi-tenant white-label behavior
