The Problem

The process breaks in handoffs.

01

Leads come from everywhere

Forms, ads, messages, and calls bring inbound across channels. But handoffs break. No clear owner. No consistent follow-up. Good opportunities go cold.

02

No unified operational queue

Spreadsheets, inbox threads, and chat updates split context. When someone asks what is next, the answer is usually scattered across tools.

03

Manual follow-up is fragile

First response slips. Reminders are missed. Tasks stay implicit. Without automation and clear accountability, lead velocity drops fast.
